Google Earth To the Rescue

Date Tue 24 January 2006 Tags life

So, it's 9PM the night before you're going to a conference. You have have no idea on how to drive to the conference. What do you do? You could try map quest or google maps. I tried that and wasn't too pleased with the results I got. Granted they both picked the fastest routes, however since the distance I'm traveling isn't that great, I'd rather have a simpler route, especially if I'm going to drive during rush hour. So on a whim I fired up Google Earth and dug through the various layer files I could turn on. To my amazement I found that it had the DC Metro. Turns out the metro is much closer to the conference than I gave it credit for. So now instead of driving during rush hour I'm gonna take the train. Thanks, Google!

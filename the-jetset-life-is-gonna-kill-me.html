The Jetset Life is Gonna Kill Me

Date Mon 13 March 2006 Tags life / work

The last two weeks have been a rush of travel and work. It all started when after a year of saying "I'll come visit you in L.A." to my friend Russell, I finally got on a plane and made good on my promise. Four days of fun in the south California sun and back to work. Followed by going out midweek, two weeks in a row and a trip to Boston this past weekend and I'm dead. Was it all worth it? Absolutely! But it's left me exhausted this morning. Luckily the day seems pretty quiet and I just have to finish up a few small things.

The only downside to the travel is that I've not gotten a chance to complete any of the projects that I'd set out to. The redhat 6.2 oldsploit stuff remains half undone and the sguil grapher remains half on the drawing board.

Luckily this is open source work done in my spare time. In terms of immediate repercusions no one is really hurt. However because I'm a fool who likes to publish this stuff, I worry. Why do I worry? Because, I don't want to be someone who says they'll do something and then doesn't do it. True I get excited alot but making good on my word is important to me.

In other news I'm starting to look for a new job. The one I have is okay, but not where I ultimately want to end up. So what's next? Where do I want to end up? I think I'd like to end up in a pentesting role or supporting a pen-testing team. The idea of having projects that are both creative and interesting as well as with a specific goal is very appealing to me. Additionally, I like bug finding in software and reverse engineering. Hopefully I can make my way there into a field that requires these. If you read my blog and are looking for someone to do that type of work my resume is posted in the papers section of this site. Note however that I'm still working on refining the resume as it's been a little while since I last updated it.
